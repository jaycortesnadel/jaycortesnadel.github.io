Q - How will I receive my swag order?
On the online order form, if you indicated “work location” as a specific office, your swag will be delivered to that office, and you will receive further instructions from Cohesity regarding pickup. If you indicated that you work remotely, and provided your mailing address, your swag will be shipped to that location.Q. Who do I contact if I have a problem ordering through the site?
Please email year-end-gift@cohesity.com.Q. What if I need to change the mailing address where my item will be shipped?
Please review your address for accuracy before submitting. There are no guarantees for address changes, post-submission. If you are in the process of moving over the next eight weeks, we encourage you to use the address of a friend or family member to avoid shipping disruptions.Q. What if I need to change my selection once I’ve submitted my order?
We are only ordering what employees select in the regions that employees select it, so we will not have a large selection of stock to modify orders. With that, selections are final, so please choose your item carefully.
